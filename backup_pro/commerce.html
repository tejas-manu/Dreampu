Commerce

Commerce education plays a very important role in the changing business world.
It is the most important key which leads to success in all sectors of economy.
Here, a student is exposed to the environment of business world. Further,
it also helps in preparing them for self-employment and developing in them, the entrepreneurial abilities.
It teaches them to face market situation and to meet the global competition. Further, there are plenty of
avenues (employment opportunities) in Commerce education. Being theoretical and practical in nature it
teaches not only technical aspects of business but also morals and ethics. Commerce education covers diversified
fields of education and research in various aspects which includes Finance, Marketing, Accounting, Human Resource
Management, Entrepreneurship Development, Taxation, Banking, Software skills, Auditing, Social Responsibilities, etc.
